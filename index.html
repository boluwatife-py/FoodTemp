Meet Elena Martinez

Master Baker & Culinary Educator

With over 15 years of professional baking experience, Elena has worked in Michelin-starred restaurants across Europe and founded three successful artisan bakeries. Her passion lies in making professional baking techniques accessible to home bakers worldwide.

Elena holds certifications from Le Cordon Bleu Paris and has been featured in Food & Wine Magazine, Bon Appétit, and The Great British Bake Off as a guest judge. She believes that great baking starts with understanding the science behind each ingredient.

"Baking is not just about following recipes—it's about understanding the magic that happens when simple ingredients transform into something extraordinary."
